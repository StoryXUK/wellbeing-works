Most wellbeing programmes fail quietly.

  • People don't know what's available.
  • Benefits are generic or disconnected.

How Wellbeing Works

  • Fund it

    Set a monthly wellbeing allowance per person.

  • Use It

    People spend it on activities that fit their lives: local, digital or together.

  • Participate

    Use-it-or-lose-it encourages real engagement, not passive access.

  • Understand it

    Participation data shows what people actually value.
