Frequently Asked Questions

Wellbeing Works is a wellbeing and lifestyle platform designed for your business or charity. It provides on-demand and live-streamed content, with access to discounted local activities and services.
The platform was originally launched as DoingOurBit and later became The Active Hub. As it evolved to support a broader range of organisations and wellbeing needs, it was rebranded as Wellbeing Works to better reflect its purpose.
Yes, absolutely. Fitness and wellbeing remain central to the platform. In addition, Wellbeing Works now includes content focused on mental wellbeing, personal development, leadership, and work–life balance.
The name Wellbeing Works reflects our mission to support healthier, more resilient people within your business or charity, offering practical wellbeing tools that work in real life.
The app is available to members, employees, volunteers, or beneficiaries of your business or charity, based on the access rules you choose to apply.
Content includes fitness classes, yoga, mindfulness, nutrition, wellbeing workshops, and personal development sessions—available live or on-demand.
Access costs are set by your business or charity. Many organisations offer the platform free to users, while some optional services or local activities may carry a small fee.
Live sessions can be joined directly through the platform. Users can view schedules, set reminders, and join sessions from any device.
Users can access discounted gyms, fitness classes, wellbeing services, and local activities provided by trusted partners in their area.
Access can be extended based on your organisation's setup — for example, to teams, members, volunteers, or selected groups.
Most sessions require little or no equipment. Any specific requirements are clearly listed in the session description.
Users receive updates through in-platform notifications and email communications, including new content, sessions, and features.
Wellbeing Works is developed by a team of wellbeing professionals and digital specialists, working with businesses and charities to support healthier, more engaged communities.
